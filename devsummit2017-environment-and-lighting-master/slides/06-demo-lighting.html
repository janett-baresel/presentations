- 2 kinds of lighting in the scene: Sunlight and Ambient light
- first Sunlight, directional lightsource, surfaces receive more light depending on whether they are facing towards the sun or not
- sun light can also cast shadows, by default it doesn't, can be activated like this
- second Ambient Light. as you might have noticed parts of the scene that are in shadow are not completely black. That's because there's still ambient light lighting those places
- Ambient light arrives from all directions evenly and thus lights every piece of surface equally.
- the amount, direction and color of these two lights depends on the specified date, which is why we can animate it
- you can see how in a city planning scenario it might be interesting to visualize the shadow cast by a new building onto neighbouring ones throughout the day
